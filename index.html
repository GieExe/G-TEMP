Guaranteed Success: Why This System Will Work

1. The Core: Industry-Leading Technology

Both recommended readers are built around the Impinj E710 chip. This is the "engine" that powers many of the world's most expensive RFID readers. You get elite performance and reliability without the premium brand price tag.

2. Professional-Grade Peripherals

The system uses components that prevent common performance issues:

  • 9dBi Circular Antenna: Provides excellent read range and reads tags regardless of their orientation.
  • Low-Loss Coaxial Cable: LMR-240 is recommended for runs up to 10m. For longer distances, an upgrade to LMR-400 is essential.

3. Flexible Power & Placement (PoE+)

The TP-Link PoE+ Injector is essential for a professional installation. It sends power and data over a single Ethernet cable, allowing you to place the reader in the optimal location for RF performance, not just where a plug is available.

4. Reliable Connectivity

A high-quality CAT5e or CAT6 Ethernet cable is required to connect the PoE+ injector to the reader. This single cable provides the stable data link for your C# application and delivers reliable power from the injector.

5. A Complete, Developer-Ready Workflow

The readers provide functional SDKs with C# demos. The Xprinter XP-H500BR (with its RFID module) and the adhesive RFID labels complete the workflow, allowing your WPF application to not only track existing tags but also to encode and print new ones on demand.

Individual Reader Recommendations

Top Choice: CHAFON CF-H815 (Impinj E710)

A proven performer that offers an unparalleled combination of price and high-end performance. It utilizes the powerful Impinj E710 chip to deliver elite read rates (900 tags/sec) for a fraction of the cost of premium brands, making it the ideal choice for high-performance projects on a budget.

Second Choice: WYUAN UHF Reader (Impinj E710)

An excellent alternative with nearly identical performance. Its key advantage is the availability of multi-port options (4, 8, or 16), making it a highly flexible and cost-effective solution for projects that may need to scale up the number of antennas.
